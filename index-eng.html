Hotel Sultan Cave Suites

Situated on the top of Aydınlı Hill, this unique hotel offers rock-cut rooms furnished with antique furniture from all over Turkey. The hotel's sun terrace offers beautiful panoramic views of the Göreme National Park.

Most of the rooms at Sultan Cave Suites are carved into the rock, providing coolness and peace. All rooms are equipped with a seating area and a modern bathroom. Most of them have a private terrace or balcony.

Cave Suites Sultan features a gourmet restaurant serving specialties from the Cappadocia region. After dinner, go out onto the terrace for a drink and watch the sun set over the village.
